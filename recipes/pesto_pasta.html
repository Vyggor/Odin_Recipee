Pesto Pasta

Tasty pasta with basil

The recipee is quite simple, delivering a healthy mediteranean pasta full of nutrients easy to prepare


What we need:


  • 500gr spaghetti
  • 250ml olive oil (preferably extra virgin)
  • 200gr parmezan cheese
  • 30gr garlic (fresh)
  • 100gr basil
  • 150gr pinecone
